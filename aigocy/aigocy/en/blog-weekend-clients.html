Your Business Has a 9-to-6 Schedule. Your Clients Don't. That's Costing You Tens of Thousands of Lei Per Month.

Friday, 7:15 PM. Mircea just got off work and has a toothache. He calls the dental clinic. No one answers. He searches Google, finds another clinic with availability, and books an appointment there. Monday morning, when you open the office, Mircea is already at the competition — and he'll never know you existed. This happens every evening and every weekend in thousands of businesses in Romania. Your clients don't follow your schedule. They call when they have time — evenings after work, Saturday mornings, Sunday afternoons. This gap is costing you tens of thousands of lei per month.

The numbers tell a story you don't want to hear

This isn't about impressions. Real industry data shows that you're systematically losing clients every evening and weekend:

  • According to data from Ruby Receptionists — based on real call monitoring of small businesses — 27% of all business calls come outside normal 9-6, Mon-Fri hours. Almost 1 in 3 clients calls when you're closed.
  • 80% of after-hours callers don't leave a voicemail — they hang up and move on. Voicemail doesn't capture clients, it loses them (source: ReadyToTalk).
  • 78% of clients buy from the first company that answers them (source: Numa Business Phone Statistics) — response speed is the most underrated competitive advantage.
  • Businesses with 24/7 availability capture 15-20% more appointments simply by taking calls outside business hours (source: Aircall AI Voice Agent Guide).
  • Real case study — The Melting Pot (PolyAI): the restaurant generated $250,000 USD in additional revenue exclusively from reservations made outside business hours, in the first 6 months after implementing the AI agent.

What does the client do when you're closed? Nothing good for you.

The client who calls on Saturday and doesn't get an answer doesn't patiently wait until Monday. They have exactly three options — and all of them cost you:

1

Reaches voicemail

Voicemail abandonment rate: over 80%. The client hangs up immediately and never calls again.

2

Calls the competition

If your competitor has a voice agent or a 24/7 open line, the client becomes theirs — permanently.

3

Sends a message on Facebook or WhatsApp

Slow or no response. The client loses trust and doesn't book an appointment.

Some industries bleed more than others

If you're in one of these fields, you lose disproportionately more from the lack of after-hours availability:

  • Medical clinics and dental offices — dental emergencies don't follow a schedule. A patient with a toothache on Saturday evening can't wait until Monday. They go to whoever answers first.
  • Beauty salons — clients are most active on Saturdays. An appointment for Saturday at 10:00 AM lost on Friday evening means an empty slot and a client who went to another salon.
  • Auto repair shops — a flat tire, a breakdown, or an urgent repair can happen Saturday morning. The client calls the first shop that answers.
  • Real estate agencies — buyers visit properties on weekends and want to call immediately when they find something interesting. If they don't get an answer, they call another agent.
  • Lawyers and consultants — legal and tax problems don't respect the 9-to-6 schedule. A client with an urgent legal-contractual issue in the evening or on the weekend seeks immediate help.
